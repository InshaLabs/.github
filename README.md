## What we do

We design and build software for founding teams, product crews, and operations that don't tolerate noise. Our work sits at the intersection of **product engineering**, **systems design**, and **interface craft** — with a growing practice in **applied AI**.

We take on **one project at a time**. It's the only honest way we know how to ship work we'd sign.

## How we work

- **Build fewer things, sharper.** We'd rather ship one feature with conviction than five with apologies.
- **Design is the interface of intent.** Pixels are the last mile; the decision tree is the real artifact.
- **Performance is a feature.** Not a finishing polish — a load-bearing commitment from day one.
- **Boring infrastructure. Loud craft.** Predictable foundations so the surface can be bold.

## The name

InshaLabs is named in homage to **Muḥammad ibn Mūsā al-Khwārizmī** (c. 780 — 850 AD), the polymath whose Latinized name became the word *algorithm*. He is our patron, our north star, and the face on our emblem.

*Inshā'* (إنشاء) — Arabic for **composition**, **construction**, **the act of bringing something into being**.
